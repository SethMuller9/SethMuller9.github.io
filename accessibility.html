Accessibility

Our Commitment

New Pathways Psychotherapy is committed to digital accessibility. We aim to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA and follow accessibility best practices so our site is usable for the widest possible audience.

What Weâ€™re Doing

  • Using semantic HTML and meaningful headings to support screen readers.
  • Providing alternative text for images and descriptive labels for form fields.
  • Maintaining sufficient color contrast and scalable text.
  • Supporting keyboard navigation and visible focus states.
  • Reducing motion where possible and avoiding auto-playing media.

Ongoing Effort

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review content and features and welcome feedback to help us improve. Some third-party tools (e.g., scheduling, maps, embedded videos) may not fully meet the same standards; we advocate for their accessibility or provide alternatives when feasible.

Need an Accommodation?

If you need information in an alternate format, assistance with booking, or another accommodation, please let us know. We will make reasonable efforts to meet your needs.

Emergency & Crisis Services

This website cannot be used for emergencies. If you or someone you know is in danger or experiencing a mental health crisis, call 911 or 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline). In Colorado, call or text 844-493-8255 (Colorado Crisis Services) or visit a walk-in center.
